ABOUT US

Know About The Adamjee Cantonment College Robotics Club

Who Are We?

We are a team of students of Adamjee Cantonment College, passionate about robotics and innovation. This club inspires students in different segments. Our goal is to inspire and engage through practical learning, teamwork, and competitions.

    WHY YOU JOIN US?

  • IMPORTANCE:
    ACCRC is one of the most important and active clubs of Adamjee Cantonment College. We work together to improve our brain and mind for the betterment of students.
  • GUIDES LIKE FRIENDS:
    Our teachers and members provide friendly guidance about robotics.
  • ONE STEP AHEAD TO CAREER:
    We teach useful robotics and tech skills that help in both career and academic life.
  • SHARING, UNDERSTANDING, TEACHING AND LEARNING:
    We share, discuss and build ideas together to learn collaboratively.
